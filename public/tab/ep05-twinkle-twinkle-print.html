Why two versions? The same melody can be played in different positions on the guitar. Version 1 stays on one string -- great for understanding how fret numbers relate to pitch. Version 2 spreads across two strings -- great for building the feel of moving between strings. Both sound the same. Try both and see which feels more natural.
How to read this: 0 = open string. Number = fret that string. Play one note at a time, left to right.
