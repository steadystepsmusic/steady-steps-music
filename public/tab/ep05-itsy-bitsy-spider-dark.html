Itsy Bitsy Spider

How to read this: 0 = open string (don't fret, just pluck). A number = fret that string at that position. Play one note at a time, left to right. Sing the melody in your head as you go -- if it sounds right, it is right.
Picking tip: Use your right-hand thumb. Pluck the B string and let your thumb land and rest on the high e. Then pluck the high e the same way -- press into it like a trampoline spring.
